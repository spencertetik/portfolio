About

I'm a Senior Motion Graphic Designer at Merge, where I've spent 4 years creating social campaigns for brands like American Express, T-Mobile, and Blue Cross Blue Shield. My daily tools are After Effects, Cinema 4D, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, and Illustrator.

Outside of Merge, I work with Everyday Astronaut — editing videos, creating animations, and helping run live rocket launch broadcasts on-site.

Every project on this page was built using AI-assisted development — primarily Claude and Gemini. I define the problem, architect the solution, and direct the AI to write the code. This is what happens when you give a designer who understands production workflows the ability to ship software.
